Powerful Features

Everything you need to manage, organize, and search your Markdown documents

🔍

Full-Text Search

Lightning-fast search across your document titles and content using SQLite FTS5, with highlighting and search history.

🤖

AI-Powered Organization

Optional summarization, Q&A, prompt extraction, and topic suggestions through your configured OpenAI-compatible provider.

📥

Bulk Import

Import Markdown from local directories with scan preview, duplicate detection, metadata extraction, and retry handling.

☁️

Cloud Storage

Store locally or use your own Qiniu bucket. You control credentials, permissions, lifecycle policy, and offsite backup strategy.

🏷️

Smart Organization

Organize with collections, tags, and AI-detected topics. Detect duplicates and manage your review queue efficiently.

🔐

Privacy First

Self-hosted solution with no telemetry. Your data stays on your device or your cloud. Complete control over your information.

💻

Desktop & Server

Native desktop apps for Windows, macOS, and Linux. Or deploy as a server for team access with full authentication.

🔄

Backup & Restore

Create manual backups, download archives, and restore with explicit confirmation. Use your scheduler and encryption tooling for production retention.

📊

Diagnostics & Monitoring

Built-in health checks, performance metrics, and diagnostic tools. Export diagnostic bundles with automatic data redaction.
