- **Depends on:** (none)

## Goal

`x/ai/resilience` stops exposing dual rate-limit and circuit-breaker primitive
stacks as equal first-class public configuration paths, and converges onto
shared `x/resilience/*` primitives with explicit compatibility adapters.

---

## Architecture Decisions

- `x/resilience` is the canonical source for reusable circuit breaker and rate
  limiter primitives; `x/ai/resilience` may compose them, but it must not end
  in a permanently dual-configured public surface.
- Compatibility-only `x/ai/circuitbreaker` and `x/ai/ratelimit` paths require
  an explicit deprecation or retention plan; silent long-term parallel ownership
  is not acceptable.
- Pull this convergence ahead of broader control-plane cleanup so the highest
  risk runtime ambiguity is resolved before follow-up docs and manifest work.
