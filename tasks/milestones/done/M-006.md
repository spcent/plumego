## Goal

Keep the published v1 state trustworthy while moving Plumego into bounded
v1.0.x maintenance and evidence-driven v1.x iteration.

## Architecture Decisions

- Treat `v1.0.0` as already published; do not rewrite, delete, or recreate the
  tag.
- Maintenance cards may fix docs, generated data, CI configuration, release
  evidence, and clear bugs only.
- Do not add stable-root public APIs in M-006 unless a dedicated symbol-change
  card explicitly authorizes the change.
- Do not promote experimental extensions from M-006 unless their evidence cards
  have two release refs, release-backed API snapshots, and owner sign-off.
- Keep each card to one primary module and commit after each completed card.
